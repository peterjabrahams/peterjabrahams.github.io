About Us

Controversial T Shirts was established in 2015 to create an awareness of the consequences and the effect of Apartheid in South Africa. Young (black) people born after 1994 know very little about the history of South Africa. Born into a democracy, they have very little understanding of what their parents faced on a daily basis.

Our Products

All our T shirts are manufactured and printed in Cape Town. Our material is 100% woven cotton, sourced from suppliers in Bellville. Our T shirts come in standard colours of White, Grey and Black, In 180 , 160 and 140 grams material. T shirt prints are done via screen printing and oven cured.
