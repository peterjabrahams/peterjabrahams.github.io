Aparheid Museum

We have been selling Controversial T shirt products since their inception in 2015. After receiving samples of the various T shirts explaining the history of Apartheid, we placed our first order of 100 T shirts. We had such a positive response from our customers that an order for 300 T shirts was placed four months later. Our order has grown since, to average 1000 T shirts per year.
